How Refund Scams Work

üìû

1. The Contact

Scammer calls/messages claiming to be from Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, or other platforms. They often have your order details to seem legitimate.

üí∞

2. The Hook

They claim you're entitled to a refund, but need to "verify" your account, pay a "processing fee", or share an OTP first.

üò±

3. The Trap

Once you share OTP, install remote access apps, or scan QR codes, they drain your bank account - often within minutes.

üö© Major Red Flags

CRITICAL - Never Do This

  • Share OTP: No company ever asks for OTP over phone
  • Pay to Receive Refund: Refunds are credited, not collected
  • Install Remote Apps: AnyDesk, TeamViewer = full device control
  • Scan QR Codes: QR codes deduct money, never add

HIGH RISK - Be Very Cautious

  • Urgency Pressure: "Act now or refund expires"
  • Unknown Numbers: Not matching official customer care
  • Processing Fees: "Pay ‚Çπ100 to get ‚Çπ1000 refund"
  • Unverified Links: Clicking suspicious links/attachments

Common Scam Types

CRITICAL

OTP Phishing

Asking for OTP to "verify" refund - this gives them access to drain your account.

CRITICAL

Screen Share Scam

Installing AnyDesk/TeamViewer for "assistance" - gives complete device control.

HIGH

Fake Processing Fee

Pay small fee to receive larger refund - you lose money, no refund received.

HIGH

QR Code Trap

Scan QR to "receive" refund - actually deducts money from your account.

HIGH

Wrong Payment Scam

Claim of mistaken payment to your account, asking to return higher amount.

MEDIUM

Fake Customer Care

Impersonating official support to collect personal/financial information.
